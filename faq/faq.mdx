<Accordion title="Which blockchains are supported?">

Currently Ethereum Mainnet & Polygon are supported. The next chains we are going to integrate are Binance Smart Chain, Optimism, Arbitrum and Goerli Testnet. If you are looking for other chains, please reach out on our [Discord Server](https://discord.com/invite/AXZTaepWGb).

</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Which filters can be used?">

As of now we support three different categories of filters:

- **NFTs**: Filter wallets that own, minted or purchased a certain NFT. Where available, the NFTs can be further filtered by their traits.

- **ERC20**:  Filter wallets that own a certain amount of ERC20 tokens.

- **Labels**: Filter wallets by insightfull labels such as `Heavy DEX Trader` or `NFT Millionaire`.

A more detailed overview can be found [here](../api-reference/segments/filters).

</Accordion>
